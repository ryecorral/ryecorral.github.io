A Father's Life..

Truly God moves in mysterious ways to touch and guide our lives....
I realized that when I received from him the greatest gift I ever received in my whole life, that is my daughter.. I grew up not knowing how to look up to my own father. I don't have much fond memories together with him. It was like, I'm just the same with other kids that don't know their fathers although he is living with us as I grew up. Then I started to build up hate and sadness towards him until my parents finally decided to split up. It was ok with me, it did not feel any different.

But one lesson stayed with me about my father, and that is to never see your family as your posession. A wife and your children are not trophies to just brag about, or subjects to command. They are gifts from God that you need to take care, love, respect and honor God's gifts by work hard for them.
This I learned after God made me experience such a terrible childhood. Back then I blame Him for everything that went wrong with our family. But now that I'm a father, I thank God so much for showing me what is wrong and taught me whats is right. Made me feel stronger love for my daughter and my wife because I don't want the same thing happen again with my own family.
